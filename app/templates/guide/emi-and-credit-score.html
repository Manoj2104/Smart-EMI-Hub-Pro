What Happens If You Miss EMI?

  • Penalty charges applied
  • Late payment reported to credit bureau
  • Credit score drops
  • Future loan approval becomes difficult

Does Prepayment Improve Credit Score?

Prepayment reduces outstanding loan but does not directly boost credit score. However, closing loans responsibly improves your credit profile.

Tips to Protect Your Credit Score

  • Set EMI auto-debit
  • Maintain emergency fund
  • Avoid multiple loans at once
  • Check credit report regularly

EMI Default vs Settlement

Loan settlement severely damages your credit score compared to regular closure. Always try to repay fully.
