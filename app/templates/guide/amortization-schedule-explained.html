What is an Amortization Schedule?

When you take a loan such as a home loan, car loan, or personal loan, banks calculate EMI using the reducing balance method. Although your EMI amount remains fixed, the portion of interest and principal changes every month.

In the early months, a large portion of EMI goes toward interest. Over time, interest reduces and principal repayment increases.

How Prepayment Affects Amortization

When you make a prepayment, the outstanding principal reduces immediately. This reduces future interest calculation and shifts amortization structure.

This is why early prepayments save maximum interest.
