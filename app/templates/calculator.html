What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount a borrower pays every month to repay a loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the lender.

Using an EMI calculator helps you understand your monthly payment based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.
